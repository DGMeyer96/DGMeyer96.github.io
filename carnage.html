About -
Carnage

A RTS developed using Unity ECS for the Gmae Programming class at CSUSB.

How To Play

Gameplay is based on popular RPGs such as Warcraft 3 which serves as this game's primary influence. There are two ways to win: points and elimination. To win by points players must hold the more victory temples longer than the other player to accumulate points. To win by elimination a player must destroy the enemy's temple in their base and thus winning the game.

Gameplay

The gmae has gamepaly similar to Warcraft 3 in terms of unit control and roles. The primary difference is that the game focuses on zone control to win similar to RTS games such as Company of Heroes. Resource control is also similar to COmpany of Heroes in that you do not have worker units being sent out to gather resources, but instead capturing key resource facilities on the map.

Development

My role on this project was Senior Programmer on the Graphics Team. I was in charge of the game's lighting and level design. I was in charge of two other student programmers for this task and with them we developed a map for the game, lit the world, setup post-processing, and lit the buildings in the world. This was done using the Lightweight Render Pipeline, Terrain Tools, and Post-Processing V2.
